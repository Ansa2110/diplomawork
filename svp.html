SportTech vs PropTech Hackathon

We invite you to the competition to develop a new project in the field of sports or real estate

Each team will have to create a project based on one of the presented cases:

  • Creation of a recruitment service
  • Development of an application or bot for collecting and accounting for funds in amateur leagues

You can:

  • learn to solve non-standard tasks
  • create a prototype in a short time
  • protect the project in front of potential partners and clients
  • upgrade your programming skills and win a cash prize

We invite you to participate:

  • Beginners and experienced developers
  • IT students
  • Testers and designers

Prize fund

  • The total prize fund is 1 million tenge.
  • 2 winning teams will receive cash prizes in the amount of 500.000₸ and 500.000₸ for the development of their projects.
