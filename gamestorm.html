SF GameStorm Night

The Game Development Center together with SF organizes SF GameStorm Night.

About competition

GameStorm Night is an event for those who love games and the creative process. Your task will be to create a cool game idea in 45 minutes in teams based on the attributes that will be given to you at the event.

Theme of games

The theme of the games is simple - to create an interesting game for SF, a Kazakh network of delicious food. And to support your imagination and creativity, SF will treat you to its most delicious pizza!

For whom

Middle level developers and above with knowledge of:

  • Python
  • JavaScript
  • Golang
  • Svelte
  • Meteor
  • React

Preferably with experience in developing or adapting computer vision algorithms

Prize fund

The prize fund of the hackathon is 2,000,000 tenge, where:

  • Prize for 1st place: 1,000,000 tenge;
  • Prize for 2nd place: 500,000 tenge;
  • Two prizes for 3rd place: 250,000 tenge each.

As well as the opportunity to get a job in a company that is a sponsor of the hackathon.
