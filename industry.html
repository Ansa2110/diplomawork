Industry 4.0 Startup Battle

The first Startup Battle from Tech Garden where you can win $22,000

About the contest

  • Industry 4.0 Startup Battle is an arena for startups to compete for the title of "Best Startup in Industry 4.0".
  • Industry 4.0 is the application of advanced technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things, cloud computing, Big Data, robotics, etc. for the digital transformation of industry

What will the startup project get?

  • Winners and ambitious participants will receive a Fast Track on all TechGarden products/programs
  • The winners will get the opportunity to pilot at Tech Garden subsoil partners
  • Financial support in the amount of $22,000

Prize fund

  • 1st place - $10,000
  • 2nd place - $7,000
  • 3rd place - $5,000
